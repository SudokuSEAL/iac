March 30, 2007

IAC Partners with NIST's MEP to Help Manufacturers Cut Energy Use

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) and the U.S. Department of Commerce's National Institute for Science and Technology (NIST) announced the creation of a partnership to help small and mid-sized manufacturers reduce energy consumption. DOE's university-based Industrial Assessment Centers (IAC) and NISTs Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) are working together to improve the energy efficiency, environmental performance and global competitiveness of these plants.

July 24, 2006
DOE Selects 26 Universities to Assess Industrial Energy Efficiency

WASHINGTON, DC - U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Secretary Samuel W. Bodman today announced the selection of 26 universities across the country for negotiation of award to set up and operate regional Industrial Assessment Centers (IAC). The centers will employ faculty and students to assist small-to-medium sized American manufacturing plants to use energy more efficiently. >>
